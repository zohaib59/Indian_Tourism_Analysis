# Indian_Tourism_Analysis
Tourism isn’t just travel in India — it’s big business and a major driver of growth.
In 2018, it contributed ₹16.91 lakh crore ($220B) to the economy (9.2% of GDP) and supported 42.6M jobs (8.1% of total employment).
Data sourced from data.gov.in
By 2019–20, tourism directly contributed 2.7% to GDP and 6.7% to employment.

Project Overview
This project analyzes foreign tourist trends using official datasets from data.gov.in.
Eight datasets power this analysis:

Dataset	Description
Tourist Arrivals 1981–2020	FTAs, NRIs, ITAs with yearly % change
Age Group Stats 2001–2019	Distribution of FTAs by age
Quarterly Trends 2001–2019	Seasonal flow of foreign tourists
India vs World Rankings	Global comparison and ranking
Region & Reasons 2019	Country-wise visit motivations
Monument Visits 2019–21	Domestic vs foreign visits to monuments
Regional Share 2017–19	Tourism market share by region
Statewise Data 2019–20	Domestic & foreign tourist counts per state
